When Good Products Go Bad

Period After Opening

The PAO symbol tells you when to throw a
product away after opening. It appears
as an open jar icon on the packaging.

Know the signs

If a product seems unusually discolored,
runny or lumpy, has separated or
developed a strange odor chances are is
out of date

What are the risks?

Serious skin problems that can occur include
allergic rashes, irritations, boils and infections.
Eye infections are the most common.
